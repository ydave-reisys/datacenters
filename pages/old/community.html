Community Support

Executive agencies are encouraged to join the Data Centers listserv by emailing listserv@listserv.gsa.gov with no subject and "subscribe datacenters" in the body (from a .gov or .mil email address only.)

In addition to addressing questions and comments via the Data Centers listserv, management.cio.gov will be updated with best practices and other resources as they become available.
