Introduction

The Data Center Optimization Initiative (DCOI) established in OMB Memorandum M-16-19 supercedes the Federal Data Center Consolidation Initiative (FDCCI) and fulfills the data center requirements of the Federal Information Technology Acquisition Reform Act (FITARA).

The DCOI requires agencies to:

  • Develop and report on their data center strategies;
  • Transition to more efficient infrastructure, such as cloud services and inter-agency shared services;
  • Leverage technology advancements to optimize infrastructure; and
  • Provide quality services for the public good.

This site strives to compile helpful links and information resources for both Federal agencies and the public regarding implementation of the DCOI.
